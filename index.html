Usage flow

1
Set your timezone using the dropdown in the top bar. This becomes your reference column (left-most, highlighted in indigo).
2
Select time slots by clicking or dragging in any column. Each row represents the same instant across all timezones.
3
Read the shadows. Selected rows are colour-coded in every other column to show what time of day that moment falls in that timezone.
4
Add team columns via the + button at the right edge of the grid.
5
Load a CSV (drag-and-drop or ↑ Load CSV), then pick a person from the Highlight dropdown to overlay their working hours.

Colour legend

Selected slot — your reference column
Shadow — 9 am–6 pm business hours in that zone
Shadow — 6 pm–10 pm evening
Shadow — outside hours likely asleep
Work-hours band highlighted person's 9 am–6 pm
Overlap your slot is inside their working hours
Midnight divider day 1 → day 2 boundary

CSV format

Two columns: name and timezone. Header row optional.

name,timezone
Alice,EST
Bob,JST
Carlos,CET
Diana,IST
Frank,America/New_York (IANA also works)
